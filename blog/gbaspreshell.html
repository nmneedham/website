Posted on 8/7/2025

Little side project I did for my wife. I gave her my beat up GBA SP, and replaced it with a shell kit I found on good ol' Aliexpress

It was a fun project, I didnt take pictures of the hinges I took out. That was the hardest part. Its not the best shell replacement but nothing beats a clean shell without teeth marks (I would get mad at Pokemon when i was younger...Dont ask)
